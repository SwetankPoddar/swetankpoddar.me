Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FriendIQ ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through the chat interface and account setup. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name and email address used for authentication.
  • User Content: Information about your friends, notes, and interactions you provide via the chat interface.
  • App Usage Data: Basic technical data needed for the app to function correctly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the Service, specifically to:

  • Create and manage your friend database and notes.
  • Analyze your chat inputs to help you remember and organize information about your social circle.
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the app.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. As a tool designed for personal memory and note-taking, your private data remains yours.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with third-party service providers (like authentication services or database providers) solely to help us provide the Service, or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can manage your data directly within the app or contact us for assistance.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@swetankpoddar.me.
